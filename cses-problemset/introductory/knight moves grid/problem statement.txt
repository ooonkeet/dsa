There is a knight on an n*n chessboard. For each square, print the minimum number of moves the knight needs to do to reach the top-left corner.

Input
The only line has an integer n.

Output
Print the number of moves for each square.

Constraints
4 <= n <= 1000

Example
Input:
8

Output:
0 3 2 3 2 3 4 5 
3 4 1 2 3 4 3 4 
2 1 4 3 2 3 4 5 
3 2 3 2 3 4 3 4 
2 3 2 3 4 3 4 5 
3 4 3 4 3 4 5 4 
4 3 4 3 4 5 4 5 
5 4 5 4 5 4 5 6
